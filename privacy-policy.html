Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 14, 2026

At Coves7, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or interact with our services.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: If you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, or other information you voluntarily provide.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about how you interact with our website, such as pages visited, time spent, browser type, IP address, and referral sources. This is done through cookies and analytics tools like Google Analytics.
  • Aggregated Data: We may compile statistical data based on user behavior to improve our content and services. This data is not personally identifiable.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To send you newsletters, updates, or promotional content (only if you opt-in). You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • To respond to your inquiries or feedback.
  • To analyze website usage and improve our content, layout, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights if necessary.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect usage data. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our site.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect certain functionalities of our website.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as:

  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics helps us understand website traffic and user behavior. Their data collection is governed by their own privacy policies (Google Privacy Policy).
  • Newsletter Services: If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email may be stored with a third-party email service provider. We ensure these providers comply with data protection standards.
  • Affiliate Partners: When you click affiliate links, vendors may collect data about your visit or purchase. This is governed by their privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who assist in operating our website or delivering services (e.g., email marketing platforms). These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • If required by law, to comply with legal processes, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Coves7, our users, or the public.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no online platform can guarantee 100% security, and we cannot be held liable for breaches beyond our control.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from newsletters or marketing emails at any time via the link in our emails or by contacting us.

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@coves7.com.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with legal requirements. Usage data may be retained indefinitely in anonymized form for analytics.

9. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, be aware that your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable laws.
